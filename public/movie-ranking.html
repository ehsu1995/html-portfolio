Best Top 3 Movie of All Time

Pirate of the Caribbean

A popular film franchise produced by Walt Disney Pictures. The series of movies revolves around the adventures of Captain Jack Sparrow, a witty and eccentric pirate played by actor Johnny Depp. The franchise includes five films released between 2003 and 2017:

Saw

A horror franchise that began with the release of the first film in 2004. The original film, directed by James Wan and written by Leigh Whannell, introduced audiences to a twisted and gruesome concept. The franchise became known for its intense and gory scenes, as well as its intricate and often convoluted plotlines.

How to Train Your Dragon

An animated film franchise based on the book series of the same name by Cressida Cowell. The franchise consists of three feature films released by DreamWorks Animation, as well as an animated television series and various other media adaptations.
